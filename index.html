Jamie Oliver

The iconic legend of Great British cookery

Here is a timeline of Jamie's career and how it all began!

  • May 1975 - Born in Clavering, Essex
  • Jan 1983 - Aged 8 he got his first job in his family's restaurant The Cricketers
  • October 1991 - Attended Westminster Catering College
  • March 1993 - Went to France to expand his pastry making skills
  • December 1995 - Worked as Head Pastry Chef for Antonio Carluccio's restaurant in Neal Street, London
  • April 1999 - The highly successful cookery series 'The Naked Chef' debuted on our television screens
  • September 2003 - Awarded an MBE from Queen Elizabeth for his service to the hospitality industry
